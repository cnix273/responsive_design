About Me

“Fashion is not something that exists in dresses only. Fashion is in the sky, in the street, fashion has to do with ideas, the way we live, what is happening.” - Coco Channel

I am an outgoing, curious, and passionate 22-year-old living in LA that has always been drawn towards art, beauty, makeup, and fashion. I believe that beauty and inspiration can be found in every facet of life, every experience.

I find joy in the subtle art of drawing on this inspiration to curate a striking yet well-balanced look, expressing myself through clothes and accessories. I love the way that accessories can perfectly round-out a look. I also am very passionate about makeup and skincare, having worked at ULTA Beauty for over a year.

I am excited to share with others this understanding of the world and the beauty within it. I seek to help others find confidence and realize their inner beauty through their outward appearance. You only get one chance at a good first impression and it starts with your outfit.

Feel free to take a look at my portfolio page to discover some of my favorite looks I have curated on myself and others. I also have a contact page for any and all inquiries. I look forward to helping you discover your true self and beauty and allowing you share that with the world through your style.
